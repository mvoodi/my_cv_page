Experience

UNICEF was the first environment where I immersed myself in project work. I worked on two projects, both aimed at benefiting society. The second project was about supporting teenagers during remote learning, helping them navigate the challenges of distance education.

I joined Enactus at university, deciding to apply and give it a try. Although my time there was short, it was an incredibly valuable experience.
